## What this is

Ask the Abolitionist is an independent project produced by
[askscottpierce.com](https://askscottpierce.com) and
[Left Join Studio](https://leftjoinstudio.com). It collects and
indexes public writings from
[abolitionistsrising.com](https://abolitionistsrising.com) and
[freethestates.org](https://freethestates.org) so readers can search
across both corpora and ask questions in plain language. We are
**not a product of, endorsed by, or speaking on behalf of** either
of those organizations.

## What we're trying to do

See every preborn human life saved. That's the whole motivation for
building this. We believe every human being — from conception onward
— bears the image of God and must be protected by law. Nothing on
this site is meant to do harm; it is meant to equip people to speak
the truth clearly, and to walk readers from wherever they stand into
the conviction that abortion must be abolished.

## Using what you read here

**The articles belong to the people who wrote them.** Their copyright
and any usage terms of the source publications still apply. We show
excerpts with a link back to the original; click through to read
articles in their intended context.

**AI answers can be wrong.** The chat box summarizes from the
underlying writings but will occasionally misquote, misstate, or
invent a detail. Every answer includes links to the source articles
the retrieval pulled from — **verify before you share in a
conversation or in print.** If an answer looks off, hit the 👎 so we
can fix it.

**Nothing here is legal, medical, or pastoral counsel.** If you're
working through grief, trauma, or shame, talk to a real pastor or
counselor in your life. If you're working on legislation, talk to a
real attorney. This site can help you think; it can't sit with you.

## Disclaimers

- The site is provided **as is, without warranties** of any kind.
  Answers stream from an LLM operating on retrieved text; neither the
  model nor the underlying corpus is guaranteed accurate, complete,
  or current.
- We don't promise uptime. This is beta — infrastructure, URLs, and
  content may change.
- We're not responsible for what other people do with information
  they read here.

## Beta

"Beta" means we are still shaping this. Article sets, voice, the
journey map, and chat behavior are all subject to revision. If
something broke, the 👎 button and the feedback row it writes is the
fastest way to tell us.
