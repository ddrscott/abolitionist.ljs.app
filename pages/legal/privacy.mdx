This is short on purpose.

## What we collect

**Feedback on AI answers.** When you click 👍 or 👎 on a chat answer,
we store four things in a Cloudflare D1 database:

- The rating (`1` for up, `-1` for down).
- The question you typed.
- The answer the AI gave.
- A timestamp.

We don't store your name, email, IP address, or any identifier that
could link the row back to you — because we never collect those in
the first place. Feedback exists so we can find where the AI gives
bad answers and improve the underlying writings or retrieval.

**Aggregate web analytics.** We run a self-hosted
[Plausible](https://plausible.io) instance at
`plausible.ljs.app`. Plausible is cookie-free and does not build
cross-site user profiles. We see page-view counts, referrers, and
scroll-depth percentages — aggregated, not per-person. We use this to
figure out which articles and journey paths people actually read, so
we know what to polish.

## What we don't collect

- **No accounts.** There is no login. There is nothing to log in to.
- **No cookies** for tracking. The site does not set any cookies at
  all for analytics or advertising.
- **No chat transcripts** beyond the feedback rows above. Questions
  you ask the chat flow through a Cloudflare Worker to Cloudflare AI
  Search and the answer streams back. Nothing is written to a
  database unless you explicitly rate the answer.
- **No third-party trackers.** No Google Analytics, Meta Pixel,
  TikTok, Segment, anything.
- **No selling, no sharing.** We don't sell data. There is no data
  to sell.

## Where things live

Everything — the static site, the Worker, the D1 feedback database,
and the AI Search instance — runs on Cloudflare, which operates from
the United States. Cloudflare's own privacy terms apply at the
infrastructure layer.

## Who sees it

The maintainers of this site, for the purpose of improving answers
and fixing gaps. That's it.

## Beta

This site is in beta. If how we handle data changes materially, this
page changes with it. The date at the bottom is the last update.
