# Start Here

This site exists for one reason: to help you see that abortion is the murder of a human being made in the image of God, and to walk you from wherever you stand today into the position that abortion must be abolished immediately, totally, and in the name of Jesus Christ.

We are not trying to win a debate. We are asking you to let Scripture come into conflict with whatever you currently believe about this subject.

## The map

Pick the box that best describes you today. Each sticking point on the map is clickable — it opens the article that addresses it. Every Christian path converges on the same destination: the position laid out in the Norman Statement. The non-Christian path terminates at the gospel.

On mobile the gates start collapsed; tap a gate to reveal its sticking points. On desktop everything is expanded by default — pan and zoom like a map.
